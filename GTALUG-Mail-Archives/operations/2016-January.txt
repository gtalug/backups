The best place to look for Debian compatible hardware would be Debian

>From personal experience I would say most computers, older than one to

with Windows.

System76 <https://system76.com/> and Dell's XPS 13 Developer Edition
<http://www.dell.com/ca/business/p/xps-13-linux/pd> can be shipped to
Canada.

If you are looking for advices for computers today that would support
Linux (or generally Linux support queries) from our entire user group
you can join our discussion mailing list:
<http://gtalug.org/mailman/listinfo/talk>. There are a lot of new and
experienced Linux users their that can answer your questions.
